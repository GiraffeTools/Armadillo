Try it on your own data

  1. Upload your surface fMRI results to NeuroVault repository.
  2. Get your unique image code: https://neurovault.org/images/[IMAGE-ID]
  3. Open the ARmadillo generator: https://www.armadillobrain.app/neurovault/[IMAGE-ID]
  4. Put the marker with QR code on your poster.
  5. Invite your audience to use their phones and wow them with the augmented reality brains!
